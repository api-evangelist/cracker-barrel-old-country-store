# Cracker Barrel Old Country Store (cracker-barrel-old-country-store)

Cracker Barrel Old Country Store, Inc. is an American chain of restaurants and gift shops with a Southern country theme, offering home-style meals and retail merchandise. Founded in 1969 and headquartered in Lebanon, Tennessee, Cracker Barrel operates more than 660 locations across the United States.
